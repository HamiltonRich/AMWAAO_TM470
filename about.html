TeamAsset Manager is an advanced asset management system designed specifically for senior management to view and make changes to the asset register. This application is a part of my OpenUniversity Degree module called TM470.

The primary goal of this application is to provide senior management with ownership and responsibility over the asset register, ensuring that the register is much more current and up-to-date. This is achieved through a direct connection to the IT department's asset management system via an API.

Features

  • View and manage the asset register in real-time.
  • Submit requisition forms for new assets.
  • Access the staff list and manage user information.
  • Add new starters and delete users.
  • Test connection to the IT department's asset management system.
  • Contact support for any issues or inquiries.

Purpose

The purpose of TeamAsset Manager is to give management ownership and responsibility over the asset register. By providing an easy-to-use interface and real-time access to the asset data, management can ensure that the asset register is accurate and up-to-date. This leads to better decision-making and resource management.
